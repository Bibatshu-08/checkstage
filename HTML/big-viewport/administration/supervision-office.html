Summary

Objectives

Primary Objectives

  1. To conserve and restore the historical and cultural arts and crafts of the Hanumandhoka Palace.
  2. To develop Hanumandhoka Palace into a museum and open it for the general public including foreigners.
  3. To manage the historical and cultural activities, festivals, and ceremonies related to the Hanumandhoka Palace.

Secondary Objectives

  1. To plan, evaluate, and monitor the Hanumandhoka Palace for development.
  2. To arrange and manage the financial resources for the Hanumandhoka Palace Museum.
  3. To manage and conserve the existing historical and cultural heritages kept at the Hanumandhoka Palace Museum according to an international standard.
  4. To procure or obtain items relating to historical and cultural arts and crafts in order to arrange them for exhibition within the Hanumandhoka Palace Museum.
  5. To work together with expert personnel in order to explore and get involved in the survey of arts and crafts and maintain progressive inventory and records of the artifacts and monuments inside the Hanumandhoka Palace Museum.
  6. To involve local communities for the expansion and development of the Hanumandhoka Palace Museum.
