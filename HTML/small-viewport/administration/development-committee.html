About

As an effect of change in the political system in the country in the year 2008 (B.S. 2067), Hanumandhoka Palace Museum Development Committee was formed with an overall goal of improving and efffectively managing the Hanumandhoka Palace Museum to an international standard in order to attract both Nepalese as well as foreign visitors. In order to achieve that goal, an executive committee has been set up consisting of members such as:

Rights and Duties

  1. Ensure adequate security arrangements for the arts, crafts, and historical and cultural monuments of the museum.
  2. Ensure timely and proper repair, renovation, and maintenance of the museum.
  3. Develop Hanumandhoka Palace for tourist attraction.
  4. Approve annual programs and annual budget.
  5. Use unnecessary space for income generation such as shops and courtyards’s rental.
  6. Undertake any other duties required for the development and management of the Hanumandhoka Palace Museum.
